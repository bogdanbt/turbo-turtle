Words per Minute (WPM): measures how fast you type. It's calculated by dividing the number of words typed (sections with letters, numbers, or underscores) by the time taken in seconds, then multiplying by 60. This helps you track your typing speed over time.

Accuracy (%): This tells you the percentage of characters you typed correctly compared to the typed text. The closer to 100%, the fewer errors you made!

Consistency: This measures how steady your typing speed is throughout the test. A higher consistency means you maintained an even pace without big speed ups or slow downs.

Keep practicing to improve your speed, accuracy and consistency. Happy typing!
